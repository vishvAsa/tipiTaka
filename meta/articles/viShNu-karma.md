Shankarasvamin and the Bengali pandit Prajnavarman's Buddhist understanding of the Buddhavatara. Prajnavarman says that just as Shiva atoned for the murder of Brahma, so too does Vishnu take birth as the Buddha to atone for murdering millions of demons. 

This is of course largely rhetorical; the focus is on exhorting Vaishnava-s to take the Buddha's actual words seriously
