From the realisation of the doctrine of the вАШsoul-less-ness of all thingsвАЩ as taught by him, follows the cessation of the whole mass of afflictions due to the notion of things having such existence. This notion of the reality of things appears in the form of вАШselfвАЩ and вАШthings related to the selfвАЩ, it is only when there are notions of вАШiвАЩ and вАШmineвАЩ that the whole mass of afflictions becomes operative.вАФThe said perception of вАШsoul-less-nessвАЩ is the enemy of this notion of reality; hence when the former becomes duly absorbed and realised, the latter disappears; therefore the entire mass of afflictions due to that notion of reality ceases, on account of the absence of its cause; and when that ceases, there is no more birth due to that. Thus there being absolute liberation from birth, this state is spoken of as the вАШfinal goalвАЩ.вАФThus the perception of вАШsoul-less-nessвАЩ is the door to unrivalled вАШgoodвАЩ.вАФAll other philosophers have held that liberation follows from the cessation of the вАШi-notionвАЩ; but if there is a вАШsoulвАЩ, this вАШi-notionвАЩ can never cease; because its efficient cause would always be there; so the objective of that notion too would not be abrogated. Question:вАФвАЬHow do you know that the mass of Afflictions arises from the notion of the real existence of things?вАЭ

The reason for this lies in the fact that the properties of the Mind cannot be pulled out like thorns and thrown away; they have arisen from the wrong notions of things, and as such they automatically cease on the cessation of their cause in the shape of the said wrong notions.
