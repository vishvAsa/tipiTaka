In the Tattvasamgraha, Shantarakshita cites a statement supposedly from the Nimitta Veda Shakha that praises Shakyamuni. These sort of subversions/reductio ad absurdums are a favorite technique of his. 

Not too long after, he argues that the Buddha-s' decision to impart teachings irrespective of jati makes them the true representations of the ethos displayed in the Gita.

He ends with contrasting statements not to criticize certain texts with the Buddhist view of investigating all statements to assert that Shramana ascetics are the true v1s.
